Oceania

Oceania is a region of diverse islands and environments from Australia's outback to New Zealand's fjords. Oceania is known for its vibrant marine life and breathtaking natural landscapes, offering stunning travel destinations such as the Great Barrier Reef and Mount Fiji's beaches. Oceania's history includes diverse cultures, European exploration, and colonial influences.
