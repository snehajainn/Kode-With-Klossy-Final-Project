Africa

Africa is a vast and diverse continent known for its rich cultural heritage and stunning landscapes, from the Sahara Desert to the Congolian Rainforests. Africa offers iconic travel destinations such as the pyramids of Egypt, Victoria Falls, and the vibrant city of Cape Town. Africa's history is marked by ancient civilizations, colonialism, and indigenous cultures and traditions.
