Asia

Asia, the world's largest continent, features diverse landscapes from the Himalayas to tropical rainforests and expansive deserts. Some popular travel destinations include the Great Wall of China, Japan's cherry blossoms, and India's Taj Mahal. Asia's history spans ancient civilizations, diverse cultures, and significant contributions to art, science, and philosophy.
