North America

North America, a continent of diverse environments from the Rocky Mountains to the Great Plains and tropical beaches, is home to some very iconic travel destinations such as New York City, Grand Canyon, and Niagra Falls. The continent offers a mix of bustling cities, natural wonders, and historical landmarks which include the history of rich indigenous cultures and the development of powerful modern nations.
