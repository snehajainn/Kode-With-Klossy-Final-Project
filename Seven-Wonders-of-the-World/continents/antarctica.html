Antarctica

Antarctica is a remote and icy continent, home to vast ice sheets, glaciers, and unique wildlife such as the Antarctic Peninsula, Antarctic Circle, and Ross Sea. Its history includes early explorations, scientific research, and international treaties dedicated to preserving its pristine environment.
